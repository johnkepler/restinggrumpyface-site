What it does

Everything you need to label a dataset well

PRECISION TOOLS

Draw it exactly the way you mean it

Boxes, polygons, and rotated boxes on a fast AppKit canvas with zoom, pan, and snapping. Drag handles, add and move polygon vertices, combine annotations, and undo anything, all without leaving the keyboard.

  • Select, Box, Polygon & Smart Select tools
  • Per-annotation label + confidence in the inspector
  • Full undo/redo and review flags
MODEL-ASSISTED LABELING

Let the model do the first pass

Built-in YOLOv3 and Apple detectors get you started instantly. Bring your own Core ML detectors (including RF-DETR raw tensors) with class mapping and confidence tuning. Or click to segment with SAM2 Smart Select.

  • Built-in YOLOv3 object detector
  • Built-in Vision detectors
  • Built-in SAM2 click-to-segment box output
  • Download and use Custom Core ML & RF-DETR detectors
DATA QUALITY

Know your dataset before you train

The Analyze Annotations report surfaces class balance, rare-class flags, geometry charts, validity checks, and duplicate images caught by perceptual hashing, even across splits. Every chart is clickable and filters the image list.

  • Class balance & rare-class detection
  • Duplicate & cross-split leakage detection
  • Clickable Swift Charts that filter your images

Round-trips with the rest of your stack

Import and export COCO, YOLO, CreateML, CVAT, and Pascal VOC, as full datasets or per-split.

Pro

Shape bigger, better datasets with Pro

Every part of labeling is free: importing, annotating, the Report, and exporting to every format. Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks the dataset-shaping extras on export and lifts the project limit.

  • Augmentation on export: generate extra training copies (flips, rotations, crops, photometric and blur effects), with every box and polygon moved to match.
  • Isolate objects: turn labeled boxes into a foldered-by-class classification dataset.
  • Export presets: save a whole export recipe and reuse it across a library in one click.
  • Unlimited projects: the free tier holds two projects per library; Pro removes the cap.

And, honestly, a warm fuzzy feeling. Keeping an app fast with thousands of images is real work, and even a small handful of people going Pro is a huge morale boost. Thank you.
